When I was young, I wanted to be anybody but myself

发表于 2016-08-11

　　The reason i forgive you is because you are not perfect.
　　You are imperfect,and so am i.
　　All humans are imperfect,even the man outside my apartment who litters.
　　When i was young,i wanted to be anybody but myself.
　　Dr Bernard Hazelhof said if i was on a desert island then i would have to get used to my own company – just me and the coconuts.
　　He said i would have to accept myself,my warts and all,and that we don’t get to choose our warts.
　　They are a part of us and we have to live with them.
　　We can,however,choose our friends and i glad i have chosen you.
　　Dr Bernard Hazelhof also said the everyone’s lives and like a very long sidewalk.
　　Some are well paved.
　　Others,like mine,have cracks,banana skins and cigarette butts.
　　Your sidewalk is like mine but probably not as many cracks.
　　Hopefully,one day our sidewalks will meet and we can share a can of condensed milk.
　　You are my best friend.
　　You are my only friend.

Ice land

发表于 2016-08-08   |   分类于 Ice land

过了这么久我才写冰岛的游记，因为我觉得这是最让我感动而且震撼的的地方，到处都是和谐与不和谐的完美融洽，冰岛之行的各种所见所闻真的让我开阔了视角，不断刷新着我对大自然的敬畏

蓝冰洞，间歇泉，黄金大瀑布，北极光 ，当你真正看到这些仿佛只有电视上才能看到的时候，脑子里只有 What the fuck!

来之前做了一些功课

冰岛位于北纬66度，靠近北极圈，冬夏两季会出现极昼极夜现象，冬天白天只有短短4~5小时，剩余全是漫长的黑夜，夏天相反，黑夜只有短短几个小时

再说说冰岛的天气和地貌，冬季的冰岛天气十分恶劣，变化无常，整个世界都背大学覆盖，夏季正好相反，到处是绿草和植被. 地貌十分奇特，有种让你感受到你身在外星球，最奇怪的是这么冷的地方，竟然全是地热水，所以说夏天的冰岛和冬天的冰岛完全是两个世界
